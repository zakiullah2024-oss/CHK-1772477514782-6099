Our Vision

To bridge the gap between advanced technology and agriculture, empowering small and marginal farmers with AI-driven insights for a better harvest.

The Problem

Every year, farmers lose up to 40% of their crop yield due to unidentifiable plant diseases. Traditional expert consultation is time-consuming, expensive, and not easily accessible to rural farmers.

Our Solution

We utilize cutting-edge **Deep Learning (CNN)** to diagnose plant diseases instantly from a simple smartphone photo. Our platform provides localized, actionable recommendations to save crops and increase profitability.
